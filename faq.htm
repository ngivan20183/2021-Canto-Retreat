問題解答 Frequently Asked Questions

下載問題解答Download FAQs (pdf)

I’m driving. How do I get there?
Here’s the address: One College Avenue, Mechanicsburg, PA 17027
I don’t have a ride. How do I get to the retreat?
If you indicated on your registration form that you needed a ride, you should be contacted.
Can I offer people ride?
Yes, you can also indicate on your registration form and provide us your phone number. Someone from the community will contact you.
Where do I go once I get at the Messiah College?
If you arrive in time during the registration period, you can head to Eisenhower Campus Center, room Brubaker “A” to check in and pick up the registration package. Please also look for room signs in the campus directing you to the registration area.
 
If you arrive after the registration period, you can head to the front lobby of Eisenhower Campus Center.
When does everything start on Saturday?
Registration starts at 12:00 PM. Lunch will not provide. Program starts at 1:30 pm.
What’s the housing situation like?
Housing is held in dorms or apartments. Each dorm room has 2-3 beds. Each dorm hallway has a bathroom for men and women. Apartments has 4-5 beds, a single bath room and a kitchen.
Do I need to bring linens or sleeping bags?
No. Linens, including blankets, pillows, and towels are provided by Messiah College. So you don’t have to bring your own linens, blankets, pillows, and/or sleeping bag.
What else should I bring?
Bible, pen, notebook, toiletries, comfortable clothes for outdoor oriented activities.
What’s going on Sunday?
We have Sunday Worship in the morning, main messages, workshops, fellowship activities, and a lot of fun time!
Will there be internet service on campus?
Yes but Internet is allowed for retreat coworkers only.
What if I need to come back to Rockville early?
Please make sure you return your key to the front lobby of Eisenhower Campus Center and let any of retreat committees know.
Can I still come if I do not sleep at Messiah College?
You can come as Commuter. More details and fee structure are described in retreat registration website and Commuter form.
If I do not use up all my meal cards, will I get any reimbursements?
No, all meal cards are final after payment.
What is the refund policy?
No refund after 4/19/2015, last day of registration.
Do we have book sales during retreat? If it does, where is it?
No, not this time.
